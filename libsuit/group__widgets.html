Detailed Description

A set of widgets.

The widgets (descendant of ui::Widget) can be divided in two groups:

  • widgets that participate in the layout: mainly ui::Container and ui::Stack (and their children classes)
  • widgets that have a graphical representation: mainly ui::Bin and ui::Leaf (and their children classes)
See also
ui::Widget, ui::Container, ui::Stack, ui::Bin, ui::Leaf
